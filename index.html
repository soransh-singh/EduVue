Simplyfing access to learning
eduVue is an Education Institute Management System (EIMS) which is designed on a modern, cloud based, multi-tenant architecture. It can be anywhere as you like, such as any geography or any devices of your choice.
Students, faculty and staff members of the institute now can perform all their duties and many more activities through a single window which includes web, mobile and other devices

Vision

Our vision is to improve access to learning for everyone using technology

Mission

To build foolproof solutions for Academic institutions by leveraging best technology and increase its adoption among stakeholders
